Cyber Kavach aims to provide people with general knowledge on cybersecurity to build a secure digital environment. Because of the increasing sophistication of cyberattacks in today's dynamic internet environment, cybersecurity knowledge is more essential than ever. Giving people the information and abilities they need to identify, mitigate, and successfully handle  is the goal that we have set. To assist users in recognizing possible hazards and maintaining their online safety, we have prepared informative blogs,  interactive activities, and real-world simulations. Our platform offers something for everyone, whether you're a beginner trying to learn the fundamentals or someone who wants to test their knowledge of cybersecurity.
